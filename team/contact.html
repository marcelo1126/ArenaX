FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your project collaboration process?

Our collaboration process includes: Requirements Discussion → Solution Design → Contract Signing → Project Development → Testing & Acceptance → Launch & Maintenance. Each phase has clear deliverables and milestones to ensure project transparency and control.

How do you typically charge?

We offer two pricing models: project-based or time-and-materials. The specific cost depends on project scope, technical complexity, and delivery timeline. We provide detailed quotes after fully understanding the requirements.

Do you provide technical support after launch?

Yes, we provide a six-month maintenance period for all delivered projects, including bug fixes, security updates, and technical consultation. After the maintenance period, you can choose to renew the maintenance contract.

Can you handle urgent projects?

Yes. We have a dedicated rapid response team that can quickly assemble a project team for development. However, urgent projects may require additional expedited fees. Please contact us to discuss specifics.
